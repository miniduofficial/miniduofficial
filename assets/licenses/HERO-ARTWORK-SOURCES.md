# Homepage hero artwork sources

The paintings used by the homepage gallery are public-domain works.

- William Blake, *The Great Red Dragon and the Woman Clothed with the Sun*, c. 1805. Source image: National Gallery of Art: https://www.nga.gov/artworks/11502-great-red-dragon-and-woman-clothed-sun
- Sandro Botticelli, *The Map of Hell*, c. 1480–1490. Source image: Wikimedia Commons: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Sandro_Botticelli_-_La_Carte_de_l%27Enfer.jpg
- Bronzino, *Venus, Cupid, Folly and Time*, about 1545. Source image: Wikimedia Commons, from the National Gallery, London: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Angelo_Bronzino_-_Venus,_Cupid,_Folly_and_Time_-_National_Gallery,_London.jpg
- Francisco de Goya, *Saturn Devouring His Son*, 1819–1823. Source image: Wikimedia Commons, from the Museo del Prado: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Francisco_de_Goya,_Saturno_devorando_a_su_hijo_(1819-1823).jpg
- Vincent van Gogh, *Starry Night Over the Rhône*, September 1888. Source image: Wikimedia Commons, from the Musée d’Orsay: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Starry_Night_Over_the_Rhone.jpg

The source pages identify the works or their reproductions as public domain.
